## The big correction: the installed wheels are not Sprintstars (GAP-17, GAP-23)

The front three-quarter photo (DOC-062d) shows **flat-five-spoke 911/Fuchs-style alloys** with black spoke recesses, polished lips, and small chrome center caps. They are not the Sprintstar pattern that the 2015 center cap order (319-655, DOC-049/057) implied in Batch 6. The evidence now reconciles cleanly:

- The **16pc chrome 14mm lug bolt set** (DOC-028) fits the installed 4-lug Fuchs-style wheels.
- The **four 5-bolt sets of 12mm** (DOC-056) and the **four Sprintstar caps** evidently belong to a wheel set that never went on the car (returned, planned, or spare).

GAP-17's resolution is corrected accordingly, and GAP-23 moves to PARTIALLY RESOLVED. Remaining: a wheel close-up for the brand stamp and physical lug count, and the tire sidewalls (GAP-12).

This is the archive working as designed: the paper trail produced a documented inference, the car overruled it, and both the original inference and the correction are preserved with sources.
