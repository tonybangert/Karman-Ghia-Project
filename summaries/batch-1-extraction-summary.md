## 2. Build Specification Cards

### Engine (SPEC-ENGINE)
VW Type 4 conversion, 1,971cc (94mm bore x 71mm stroke, called "2000cc" in the build notes). 8.6:1 static compression at .028 deck height with 61cc effective chambers. Head work: valve job, opened for 94mm cylinders, flycut .050, spark plug inserts (one head carries a 2nd-oversize insert), Type 4 rocker spacer kit to restore geometry. Engle camshaft (grind unknown). Dual 44mm Webers. Mechanical fuel pump blocked off. Type 4 spin-on oil filter. Alternator on Raby billet stand with integrated oil filler. K&N oiled-gauze air filters implied by the recharge kit purchase.
Sources: DOC-001, DOC-003, DOC-004, DOC-007

### Cooling (SPEC-COOLING)
Jake Raby DTM (Down The Middle) Stage III system: fiberglass shroud routing air evenly to all four cylinders, allowing Type 4 installation in a Type 1 engine bay without cutting. Kit includes oil cooler adaptor, 6 pc waterjet surround tin, air sealing kit, billet conversion pulley, DTM fan belt, air hose.
Sources: DOC-001, DOC-004

### Transmission (SPEC-TRANSMISSION)
Built by Steve Hansen. Freeway Flyer configuration (taller cruising gears). Ratios unknown.
Source: DOC-004

### Front Suspension / Steering (SPEC-FRONTEND)
2.5" drop spindles, adjustable front beam, KYB shocks.
Source: DOC-004

### Brakes (SPEC-BRAKES)
Front disc conversion. KGPR calipers w/pads (615-107, 67-74 fitment adapted) and TRW master cylinder (611-015, 67-74 fitment adapted), both annotated "customer says it will fit" on the invoice, documenting deliberate cross-year adaptation. 7mm hydraulic hose, 2m.
Sources: DOC-002, DOC-004

### Exhaust (SPEC-EXHAUST)
Ceramic coated system. Manufacturer unknown.
Source: DOC-004

### Interior (SPEC-INTERIOR)
JBugs Deluxe Velour/Tweed Interior Kit (SKU 122). Grey Velour #56 headliner (20-1510-V 56). Charcoal flat-weave 19 pc carpet (34-F1523-607). Custom 56-61 style door panels with 12" inserts (14-1522-CUST). Front seat pads 43-1515 x2, rear pad 43-1518, upholstery 44-1523 (66-67 sedan, 12" insert, 56-61 style).
Sources: DOC-005, DOC-006
