## GAP-02 resolved: the restoration's start date is July 29, 2013

The complete, uncropped Raby Enterprises receipt arrived: **Sales Receipt #1878, dated 7/29/2013**, from Sales Order #94, cashier "Jake" (plausibly Jake Raby himself), with a handwritten "will be shipped as soon as possible" note beside the DTM Stage III line. This is now the earliest dated document in the archive and it anchors the whole story: the engine program started with the cooling system purchase, and the heads went to NV Automotive in Las Vegas two weeks later (August 13, 2013). The kit shipped to Hurricane UT, confirming Mahan was based in Utah from the very beginning of the build, with the Vegas head work a road trip away.

The documented restoration window is now **July 29, 2013 to May 31, 2018**, with a coherent arc: engine program launch (Jul-Aug 2013, Utah/Vegas), body and paint (2014, Washington County Collision, Hurricane UT), assembly (2015-2016, Henderson NV), maintenance (through 2018).

## DOC-046: the body reseal motherlode

West Coast Metric invoice 120072, January 20, 2015, four pages, $364.45, 28 lines. Each page was photographed twice; the dedupe matched them on invoice number. The order is anchored by two kits exploded into components: the **141-021C Body Restoration Set for Ghias 65-69** (turn indicator, tail light, headlight ring, decklid, hood, license light seals) and the **143-018A quarter window popout seal kit**. Around them: an 8-fuse fuse box with cover (correct 1961-66 spec), the **Karmann side body badge emblem**, a full wiper system refresh, the German dual horn boot, and glove box parts. Placed two days before the January 22 activator and hardware runs, this order opens the Henderson assembly phase.

## DOC-047: the wiring evidence GAP-12 was waiting for

McFadden-Dale, February 2, 2015, signed James Mahan: five spools of primary wire (12ga and 16ga, four colors), 100 cable tie mounts, grommets, and stainless hardware. No complete harness appears anywhere in the archive, so the electrical work was hand-run circuit by circuit. GAP-12 is now partially resolved (tires remain the last undocumented category).
