## Two cornerstone provenance documents

**1. The handwritten cost ledger (DOC-043).** Two columns in the restorer's hand, "Karman Ghia" and "2000cc Engine," and the first line answers a question we never had a document for: **the car was purchased for $500**. The body column itemizes media blast, Bondo, Dolphin Glaze, fiberglass, replacement nose/fenders/hood, and two primer stages. The engine column itemizes the bottom end that GAP-09 was hunting: 94mm pistons and cylinders ($169), crankshaft ($60), bearings, lifters, rockers, gasket set, alternator, oil pump milling, a **lightened flywheel** (new spec detail), and "$1000 Shroud assembly," which matches DTM Stage III pricing and corroborates the Raby packing list. The ledger sums to roughly $2,994 including the car.

**2. The paint trail is solved (GAP-13).** Two documents close it from both ends. The Auto Paints Plus receipt (June 19, 2014, Hurricane UT) is the paint-day materials purchase: epoxy primer/sealer, a $193 mixed color in the "Dimentions" system referenced **"Mixwa208v,"** slow activator, five liters of urethane clear, and reducer, all sold to **Washington County Collision**, identifying the body shop. Then the Boulder Auto Parts invoice (January 12, 2015) supplies the second formula reference: **Nason Ful-Thane BC/CC mixed to GM formula 3295**, ten days before the SelectClear activator we logged in Batch 2. Translating wa208v / GM 3295 into a color name is the last step (GAP-21).

## The geography now reads correctly

Batch 4 reverses our earlier assumption about the address sequence. West Coast Metric shipped to **Hurricane UT in April and May of 2014**, and the paint was bought there in June 2014. Henderson NV begins January 2015. So the build flow was: Las Vegas head work (Aug 2013), Utah body-and-paint phase (2014, Washington County Collision), Henderson assembly phase (2015-2016), and ongoing maintenance (2018). This also re-dates the Raby DTM kit, which shipped to the Hurricane address: best estimate is now the 2014 Utah phase, which slots perfectly after the 2013 head work.

- **Door resealing documented end to end:** the complete KGPR door rubber kit (18 lines, April 2015), backordered main door seals fulfilled eight days later, with the West Coast Metric striker kit and the Permatex weatherstrip adhesive purchases bracketing it.
